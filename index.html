Ahmedur Rahman Shovon

Postdoctoral Appointee
Mathematics and Computer Science Division
Argonne National Laboratory

I am a Postdoctoral Appointee in the MCS Division at Argonne National Laboratory, working with Dr. Swann Perarnau. My research focuses on designing system software and runtime infrastructure for energy-efficient HPC platforms to support large-scale scientific data streaming workflows. During my PhD with Dr. Sidharth Kumar, I developed the first multi-node, multi-GPU Datalog engine and applied topological data analysis to brain imaging.

My research interests range across high-performance computing (HPC), data analytics, and cloud computing, with a broader goal of enabling scalable, energy-aware scientific discovery.
