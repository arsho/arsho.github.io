Privacy Policy

By Ahmedur Rahman Shovon

Published January 21, 2022, Updated September 23, 2025

It’s quite basic at the moment. We use the third-party, Google analytics and Google AdSense, which will likely collect information. But it’s worth reading their privacy policy for more information on that. Besides, these third parties, we don’t use cookies, store, or collect information.

Effective date

The effective date of this privacy policy is January 01, 2022.
